Bios

Dr. Jin’s research interests include cyber-security, modeling and simulation of large-scale systems, software-defined networking, and cyber-physical system security and resilience. His research team received several Best Paper Awards including the IEEE SmartGridComm in 2020, ACM SIGSIM-PADS in 2012, 2015, and 2019, and IET Cyber-Physical Systems: Theory & Applications in 2018. His research has been funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF), Air Force Office of Scientific Research (AFOSR), National Security Agency (NSA), Department of Energy (DOE), Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL), and various industrial research collaborations. He is a recipient of the AFOSR Young Investigator Program (YIP) Award in 2017 and the CS Teacher of the Year Award at IIT in 2015.
