Bios

Ankush is the Director of Advanced Analytics, responsible for collaborating with leaders and experts across Exelon’s six utility companies to develop and deliver a robust Grid T&D and Safety Advanced Analytics program. Ankush co-founded Exelon’s premiere Analytics Academy program which is helping enable awareness and adoption of analytics across organizations to accelerate development of an analytics driven culture. He spent 12 years at Exelon in various Analytics leadership roles where he was responsible for shaping the strategy and execution of Exelon’s Audit & Controls’ data analytics and IT Audit function. He previously worked at Fannie Mae and TD Ameritrade. Ankush leads Utility Analytic Institute’s Safety Analytics Community initiative, serves as Chairperson on Association of Edison Illuminating Companies’ (AEIC) Analytics Council, and is on the advisory board for Grid Forward. He has two young daughters and is passionate about increasing engagement of girls in STEM programs and technology careers. Ankush is a University Gold Medalist from Louisiana State University, where he earned his bachelor’s and master’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering. He also spent time conducting research on Memory Storage Estimation and Optimization for Embedded Systems.

Abstract

Exelon’s goal to become the utility of the future has led to a strategic initiative to accelerate our analytics maturity and culture, and empower employees to make data-driven decisions. As a result, the firstof- its-kind Analytics Academy was launched to upskill the organization to meet the future’s critical digital and insight-driven needs. The Academy provides a university experience, providing employees with coaching and mentoring, curated advanced analytics training, and connecting them with a vast community and network of analytic professionals and leaders applying analytics capabilities to solve high-priority business problems.
