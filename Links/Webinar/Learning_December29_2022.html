Bios

Prof. Dr. Mario Paolone received the master and PhD degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Bologna, Italy, in 1998 and 2002. From 2005 to 2011, he was assistant professor in power systems with the University of Bologna. Since 2011, he has been with the Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland, where he is Full Professor. His research interests focus on power systems with particular reference to real-time monitoring and operational aspects, protections, dynamics and transients. Dr. Paolone's most significant contributions are in the field of PMU-based situational awareness of active distribution networks (ADNs) and in the field of exact, convex and computationally-efficient methods for the optimal planning and operation of ADNs. Dr. Paolone is Fellow of the IEEE.

Abstract

In this presentation we will share lessons learned on how we are working to upskill our talent, developing an analytics community of practice, extracting hidden value from our internal talent, and showcase success stories.
